Laurin Hirsch
Hey, I'm a 22 year old munich based photographer studying design an photography.
I've been taking photos since the first time I first took interest in my dads old camera at age 14. Since then, taking photos has never left my mind and I couldn't imagine a day passing without it playing a part. It is entangled with my every activity and the culture I feel at home at. I use it to document life and humans within it.
The analog process inluding darkroom printing has become an integral part to my workflow and the look I produce over the last couple of years. I'm currently finishing by bachelors degree.
